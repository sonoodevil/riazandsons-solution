Our Story

Built on Legacy,
Driven by Excellence

Riaz & Sons Solutions (Pvt.) Ltd was established in 1965 and has been offering its services in the field of Civil, Electrical, Mechanical & HVAC. In the past 60 years the company has developed a unique portfolio to operate as performance leader to achieve operational excellence.

The company has developed industry-leading customer satisfaction and superior financial performance. In line with our growth strategy we have set core values that are shared by each and every associate.

Today, under the leadership of Adnan Riaz, Riaz & Sons Solutions continues to expand — elevators, escalators, generator supply, HVAC, logistics and MEP contracting — serving government and private sector clients across Pakistan from offices in Lahore, Islamabad, Karachi, Multan and Peshawar.
