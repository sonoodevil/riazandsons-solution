Our Story

Built on Legacy

Riaz & Sons Solutions (Pvt.) Ltd was established in 1965 and has been offering services in Civil, Electrical, Mechanical & HVAC. In the past 60 years the company has developed a unique portfolio to operate as performance leader to achieve operational excellence.

In line with our growth strategy we have set core values — Safety First, Team Work, Respect, Commitment and Building a Better Future — shared by each and every associate.

Today, under Adnan Riaz, we continue to expand — elevators, HVAC, generators, logistics and MEP contracting — with offices in Lahore, Islamabad, Karachi, Multan and Peshawar.
