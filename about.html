Our Story

Six decades of engineering excellence — founded in 1965, built on trust, driven by quality.

Founded 1965

Built on Legacy

Riaz & Sons Solution carries forward a proud engineering legacy established in 1965. For over 60 years, this company has been offering services in Civil, Electrical, Mechanical and HVAC — developing a unique portfolio as a performance leader in Pakistan's engineering sector.

In the past 60 years, the company has developed a unique portfolio to operate as performance leader to achieve operational excellence, industry-leading customer satisfaction and superior financial performance.

Today, under the leadership of Adnan Riaz, the company continues to expand — elevators, escalators, generator supply, HVAC and professional logistics serving government and private sector clients across Pakistan.
