Who We Are

Our Story

Riaz & Sons Solution is a complete engineering solutions company based in Pakistan, specializing in elevators, HVAC systems, diesel generators, and professional logistics. We serve both government and private sector clients with the highest standards of quality, safety, and reliability.

Our mission is simple: deliver engineering solutions with reliability, professionalism, and uncompromising quality — from our facility to your site. We work around the clock to ensure every project moves forward safely, efficiently, and on schedule.

With successful contract awards including City Center Commercial Tower, Peshawar (PDA) — where we were selected as the primary partner to manage, service and upgrade all vertical transportation systems — we have established ourselves as a trusted name in Pakistan's engineering sector.

Every piece of equipment we handle is carefully transported using industry best practices, ensuring it reaches its destination in perfect condition. From passenger elevators to heavy diesel generators — we power industries and deliver reliability.
