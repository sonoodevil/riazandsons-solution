Our Story

Built on Legacy,
Driven by Excellence

Riaz & Sons Solution carries forward a proud engineering legacy established in 1965. For over 60 years, this company has been offering services in the field of Civil, Electrical, Mechanical and HVAC — developing a unique portfolio as a performance leader in Pakistan's engineering sector.

Today, under the leadership of Adnan Riaz, the company continues to grow — expanding into elevators, escalators, premium generator supply, and professional logistics. We serve government and private sector clients with the same commitment to quality, safety and reliability that has defined this name for six decades.

Our mission: deliver engineering solutions with reliability, professionalism, and uncompromising quality — from our facility to your site. We work around the clock to ensure every project moves forward safely, efficiently and on schedule.
